### 2. **What Data We Collect**

We collect the following types of data:

#### **A. Personal Data**
1. **Account Information:** Name, email address, and contact details of hosts using our service.  
2. **Payment Information:** Payment details (e.g., credit card information) for processing transactions related to Shield devices and service subscriptions.  

#### **B. Network and Usage Data**
1. **Network Metadata:**  
   - Device connection/disconnection times.  
   - MAC addresses (anonymized via hashing).  
   - IP addresses (not stored in a way that identifies individuals).  
2. **Network Health Data:**  
   - Wi-Fi signal strength.  
   - Bandwidth usage (upload/download speeds).  
   - Latency, jitter, and packet loss.  
   - Device connectivity issues (e.g., frequent disconnections).  
3. **Device Usage Data:**  
   - Types of devices connected (e.g., smartphones, laptops, smart TVs).  
   - Aggregated usage patterns (e.g., peak connection times).  
   - Duration of device connections.  

#### **C. Data for Troubleshooting and Support**
- **Error Logs:** Diagnostic information to identify and resolve technical issues.  
- **Configuration Data:** Settings and configurations of the Shield device for troubleshooting.  
- **Customer Support Requests:** Information provided by you when contacting support (e.g., descriptions of issues).

### 3. **How We Use Your Data**

We process your data for the following purposes and legal bases:

#### **To Provide the Shield Service**
- **Detecting Overstays:** Monitor network traffic to identify devices connected longer than the booked stay duration.  
  - **Legal Basis:** Legitimate Interests (GDPR Article 6(1)(f)) – To protect our customers’ property from unauthorized use.  
- **Scheduling Cleanings:** Identify when guests leave by monitoring device disconnections.  
  - **Legal Basis:** Legitimate Interests (GDPR Article 6(1)(f)) – To help customers manage their properties efficiently.  
- **Monitoring Arrivals:** Follow up with hosts if guests do not arrive as scheduled.  
  - **Legal Basis:** Legitimate Interests (GDPR Article 6(1)(f)) – To ensure the security of our customers’ property.  
- **Network Health Monitoring:** Provide real-time insights into Wi-Fi performance to ensure a seamless guest experience.  
  - **Legal Basis:** Legitimate Interests (GDPR Article 6(1)(f)) – To improve service quality and customer satisfaction.  

#### **To Manage User Accounts and Provide Support**
- Process account information to manage your account and provide customer support.  
  - **Legal Basis:** Contract (GDPR Article 6(1)(b)) – Necessary to perform our contract with you.  

#### **To Process Payments**
- Process payment information to complete transactions for Shield devices and services.  
  - **Legal Basis:** Contract (GDPR Article 6(1)(b)) – Necessary to perform our contract with you.  

#### **For Troubleshooting and Customer Support**
- Use error logs, configuration data, and customer support requests to resolve technical issues and improve service quality.  
  - **Legal Basis:** Legitimate Interests (GDPR Article 6(1)(f)) – To provide effective customer support and troubleshoot issues.  

#### **For Research and Development (R&D)**
- Use **anonymized and aggregated data** to:  
  - Identify trends in device usage and network performance.  
  - Develop new features and improve the Shield service.  
  - Conduct market research and innovation.  
  - **Legal Basis:** Legitimate Interests (GDPR Article 6(1)(f)) – To innovate and enhance our products.  
  - **Safeguards:** Data is anonymized (e.g., MAC addresses hashed, IP addresses masked) and aggregated to prevent identification of individuals. Users can opt out of R&D data use by contacting us at **carl@gueststatus.com**.  

#### **To Comply with Legal Obligations**
- Process your data to meet legal or regulatory requirements.  
  - **Legal Basis:** Legal Obligation (GDPR Article 6(1)(c)).

### 4. **How We Handle and Protect Data**

To ensure GDPR compliance, we implement the following measures:

#### **A. Local Data Processing**
- Most data is processed locally on your Shield device and never leaves your network.  
- Only specific data required for troubleshooting, customer support, or R&D is transmitted to us.  

#### **B. Anonymization**
- **MAC Addresses:** Hashed using a one-way hashing algorithm (e.g., SHA-256) immediately upon collection.  
- **IP Addresses:** Not stored in a way that identifies individuals.  
- **Aggregated Data:** Combined across multiple properties to prevent identification of individual users.  

#### **C. Data Minimization**
- Only collect data necessary for the stated purposes.  
- Avoid collecting sensitive or unnecessary personal data.  

#### **D. Data Retention**
- **Network Data:** Retained for **90 days**, then anonymized or deleted.  
- **Network Health Data:** Retained for **24 months** for R&D purposes, then anonymized or deleted.  
- **Usage Data:** Retained for **24 months**, then anonymized or deleted.  
- **Account Information:** Retained while your account is active. Deleted within **90 days** after account closure, unless required for legal reasons.  
- **Payment Information:** Retained as required by law (typically **5 years**).

### 7. **Your Rights Under GDPR**

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your data.  
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.  
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data under certain circumstances.  
- **Restriction of Processing:** Limit how we use your data.  
- **Data Portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format.  
- **Objection:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests.  
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time (if applicable).  

To exercise your rights, contact us at **carl@gueststatus.com**. We will respond within one month.
